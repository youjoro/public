My OJT Experience

For the upcoming practicum that I was gonna take, I have already sent a few emails to different companies, asking whether their internship position was available. But with the help of a friend I managed to get accepted to Integrated Micro-Electronics Inc.

I started my On-The-Job Training on May 2, 2022, where I chose to be assigned in Information/ Infrastracture Security

Since I am kinda new to the subject, they told to learn about Virtual Machines and the Microsoft Server OS, which will be the two things I would be doing during my OJT. They also told me to learn both the ISO 27001 standard and the NIST Framework so I could better understand what I needed to do to make sure the servers I set up are secure.

I was also assigned in the IT Helpdesk department where I usually observe what they do, do basic app installations, troubleshooting and PC and LAN Cable Setup.

Currently I still lack enough hours to finish my OJT, but it also means I can learn more about IT security and its other parts that makes their other systems secure.

I would be also learning about Cloud Security, Endpoint Security, and Sharepoint security at some time in the future.
